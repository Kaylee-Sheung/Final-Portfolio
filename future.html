Emerging Future

Description

In this project, I decided to work with application and UI design, to find some possibilities in the future.

This is a group project, and I worked with Elena Peng. We imagined a future where people are struggling with hunger issues, but with the development in technology and food strategies, people invented online food shopping applications for most people to solve the food supply problems.

This application has such functions, people can both shop and “create” food, you can create your own recipe for the “Future food” with different flavors and ingredients provided in different categories, you can also sell your own recipe on “market” section in the app. We also decided to add some special activities during festivals so that customers can participate and get some rare flavors/ingredients.
